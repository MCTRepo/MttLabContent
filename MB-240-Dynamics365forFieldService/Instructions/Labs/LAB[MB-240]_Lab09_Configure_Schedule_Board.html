Practice Lab 9 - Configure the Schedule Board

Exercise 1 - Create and configure a new schedule board

You can create new schedule boards and configure the for your requirements. In this exercise we will create and configure a schedule board.

Task 1 – Create a schedule board

  1. In the Dynamics 365 Field Service app, click the Service area in the bottom-left of the sitemap, and in the Scheduling group select Schedule Board.

  2. Click the + icon in the top-left of the schedule board to add a new schedule board tab.

  3. Enter [your prefix ex. mollyc] + Board for Board Name.

  4. Select Everyone from the Shared with drop-down field.

  5. Select the Schedule Assistant tab.

  6. Enter c6fcb3 for Available color.

  7. Select Start of travel from the Book based on drop-down field.

  8. Select the Board colors tab.

  9. Enter c6fcb3 for Not booked.

  10. Enter bf2626 for Current timeline.

  11. Select the Other tab.

  12. Enter 10 for Requirement page count.

  13. Enter 12 for Resource page count.

  14. Enter 4 for Number of days displayed in hours view.

  15. Click Add.

  16. Select the [your prefix] Board tab.

  17. Click on the Scheduler Settings gear icon on the Schedule Board.

  18. Select your Time Zone.

  19. Select 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM for Working time.

  20. Select 30 minutes for Time resolution.

  21. Click on the Filters icon.

  22. Select User from the Resource Types drop-down field.

  23. Click Apply.

  24. Click Save as default.

  25. Close the Filters pane.
